Open Aviation Components

Interactive components for aviation training, implemented as standard web components (custom elements). Framework-agnostic — use them in plain HTML, Vue, React, Svelte, Slidev, or anywhere else custom elements are supported. Licensed under MPL-2.0; fork and contribute freely.

Components

  • FourForces 3D visualization of lift, weight, thrust, and drag with a live physics model.
  • ClimbPerformance Interactive thrust and power vs airspeed charts showing Vx (best angle) and Vy (best rate) of climb.
